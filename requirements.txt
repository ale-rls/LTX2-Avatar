                          # package". CPU build is plenty (the VAD model is tiny). Do NOT
                          # rely on comfy_mtb's onnxruntime-gpu: it can target a different
                          # CUDA major than torch (we hit libcudart.so.13 vs CUDA-12) and
                          # then fails to import. setup.sh force-installs the CPU build.
